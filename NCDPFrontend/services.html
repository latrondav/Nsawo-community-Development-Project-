Our Services

Empowering communities through impactful initiatives

SaG Project

Save a Girl Project: Providing reusable, hygienic, and environmentally friendly sanitary kits to girls in rural schools, keeping them in school and empowering their futures.

Educational Support

Providing science equipment to schools like Ssaku Secondary School and ensuring electricity access to schools, like Balita Lwogi Primary School, that cater to children with special needs.

Community Development

Engaging in projects that enhance the well-being of the community, such as refurbishing homes for families in need, and providing essential items like shoes and hygiene kits to schools in Luwero district.

Nsawo Community Development Project

At Nsawo Community Development Project, we are driven by a passion for positive change and community empowerment. Our mission is to make a lasting impact on the lives of individuals, especially in Luwero district, through various initiatives aimed at education, hygiene, and inclusivity.
