About Us

Empowering Communities, One Initiative at a Time

At Nsawo Community Development Project, we are driven by a passion for positive change and community empowerment. Our mission is to make a lasting impact on the lives of individuals, especially in Luwero district, through various initiatives aimed at education, hygiene, and inclusivity.

Read More

Community Development

We believe in building strong communities. Through our SaG Project (Save a Girl), we provide reusable and environmentally friendly sanitary kits to girls in rural schools, ensuring their uninterrupted education.

Educational Support

Our commitment to education is evident in our efforts to equip schools like Ssaku Secondary School with science equipment and providing electricity to Balita Lwogi Primary School, an integrated school for children with special needs.

Home Rehabilitation

We go beyond the classroom by refurbishing homes, such as Angel's grandmother's home, ensuring a safe and comfortable environment for families facing challenges.

Hygiene and Well-being

We care about the well-being of children, demonstrated through initiatives like distributing hygiene kits and shoes to schools like Bukolwa Church of Uganda Primary School and Nsawo Church of Uganda Primary School.
